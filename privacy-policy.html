Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

SocialHub ("we", "our", "us") is an internal analytics tool operated by ULTIMATE LIFE Inc. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data accessed through the TikTok API.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following data from TikTok through authorized API access:

  • Public profile information (display name, avatar, bio)
  • Account statistics (follower count, video count)
  • Video metadata and performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares)

3. How We Use Data

Collected data is used solely for internal business analytics purposes to monitor and improve our own social media performance. We do not sell, share, or distribute any collected data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on secure, access-controlled internal servers. Access is limited to authorized employees only.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as it is needed for analytics purposes. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

6. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at our corporate office.
